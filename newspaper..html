Telangana State

Telangana is a state in southern India, formed on June 2, 2014, after being part of Andhra Pradesh. It's known for its diverse culture, rich history, and growing economy, particularly in manufacturing and IT services. Telangana is situated on the Deccan Plateau and is drained by the Godavari and Krishna rivers. Its capital and largest city is Hyderabad.
